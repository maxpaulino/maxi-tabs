## Notes & Performance Tips

- **Tuning:** Standard (E A D G B E)  
- **Capo:** No capo  
- **Techniques:** Hammer-ons, slides, fingerpicking (best played without a pick)  
- **Tone / Effects:** Clean, slightly reverb-heavy tone  

---

## Notation Key

- **h** = Hammer-on  
- **p** = Pull-off  
- **b** = Bend  
- **/** = Slide up  
- **\** = Slide down  
- **x** = Mute / dead note  
- **(n)** = Ghost note / optional note
