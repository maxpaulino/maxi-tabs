## Notes & Performance Tips

* **Tuning:** DADF#BE (Open D Alternate)
* **Capo:** None
* **Tempo (BPM):** Moderate / downtempo
* **Strumming Pattern:** Primarily fingerpicked with ambient timing
* **Techniques:** Hammer-ons, pull-offs, slides, bends, harmonics
* **Tone / Effects:** Warm clean tone, subtle reverb and delay; slight overdrive during chorus
